About College Vision and Mission Latest Updates

Balaji Institute of Technology & Science is the one of the best Engineering college in warangal district, popularly known as BITS, was established by Maheshwara Educational Society in 2001 at Laknepally Village near Narsampet beside Warangal-Narsampet Main Road in an area of 35 acres with an in take of 180. The place is well connected by road and train and is only 20 minutes drive from Warangal.

To be a centre for excellence in preparing the graduates professionally committed, intellectually adept and ethically balanced with high standards by imparting quality education with international standards to excel in their career to meet the challenges of the modern world and adapt to the technologically changing environment.

To strive hard to produce technically trained human resources to serve the present and future global needs by providing quality education
